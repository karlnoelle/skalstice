What is Skålstice?

A bi-annual tradition on the Summer and Winter Solstice (or closest weekend) that celebrates life, changing seasons, old and new friendships, and the future.

Where’s this party held?

In Minneapolis, Minnesota. For privacy reasons, contact one of our hosts for the address.

This Winter we’re introducing a few activities that may require a towel.
